The Operating Thesis
The Operating Thesis
One question decides the channel
Can you build a named list of this investor? If yes — we hunt them with AI-powered outbound. If no — if they are an anonymous searcher with a problem — we make sure VisionWise is the answer they find.
Engine 1 · Inbound

SEO / GEO

Catch the raised hand

For investors you cannot enumerate — event-triggered, self-educating people searching Google and asking AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews).

  • Validation layer for whales who Google VWC after a touch
  • Educational + GEO capture for searcher personas
  • Runs in the SEO repo · compliance-gated (RIA)
Engine 2 · Outbound

AI Lead Gen

Build the list. Reach out.

For finite, enumerable, reachable universes — family offices, RIAs, apartment owners, operators — identified, enriched, and engaged across digital and analog touches.

  • Apollo · Hunter · ATTOM · BatchData · OpenCorporates
  • LinkedIn Sales Navigator · SEC IAPD · FINTRX
  • AI scoring + personalization → HubSpot → SDC closes

VWC's 2026 goal is $10M raised ($25M in 2027), anchored by a $30M revolving line of credit from a single family office. That anchor is pure outbound — SEO will never land it. The two engines are complementary: outbound creates awareness; SEO/GEO validates and captures the searchers outbound can't see.

Guardrail — VisionWise sells securities under Reg D 506(c)

VWC is not a registered investment adviser or broker-dealer — it is a private sponsor of its own offerings. Every external word is a securities-offering communication under the federal anti-fraud rules (Rule 10b-5). Under Rule 506(c), general solicitation is permitted, but every investor's accredited status must be verified (reasonable steps, not self-certification) before they invest. Accredited investors only. No misleading or cherry-picked performance, no "safe/guaranteed," no forward promises without review.

Step1–2

Identify & enrich

Pull the finite universe (IAPD, FINTRX, ATTOM). AI scores each record for ICP fit; Apollo/Hunter/BatchData append + verify contacts. 100 verified targets beat 5,000 unverified.

Step3

Personalize at scale (AI)

AI drafts persona-calibrated, SDC-voice copy referencing the specific firm/property/event — reviewed, never auto-sent. Compliance-checked templates.

Step4

Multi-touch sequence

LinkedIn connect (no pitch) → value email → proof (track record / case study) → invite. Email infra on a dedicated domain (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) for family-office deliverability.

Step5

Route & close

Engaged leads flow to HubSpot; SDC and team take the meeting. AppFolio handles post-investment IR — we don't duplicate it.

Guardrails & Next Steps
Guardrails
Compliance is the strategy, not a footnote

Hard rules (VWC sells 506(c) securities — not an RIA)

  • No performance claim, forward-looking return, or "safe / guaranteed / no-risk" language without outside-counsel review
  • 506(c): general solicitation permitted, but every investor's accredited status must be verified (not self-certified)
  • Accredited-investor verification gates any offering action; case-study performance stays counsel-reviewed
  • No aggressive tracking / retargeting to consumer audiences; first-party, privacy-grade only
  • Track record shown in full — including the disclosed loss; no cherry-picking

What we need from VisionWise

  1. Stand up accredited-investor verification (506(c)) — the gate that lets the SEO capture track go live.
  2. Ratify persona priorities — confirm P1 (LOC) + P6 + the P3/P5/P8 split as the Q1 focus.
  3. Grant tool + data access — HubSpot, LinkedIn admin, FINTRX budget, and the 15k-owner list.
  4. Name the compliance counsel for the content-review SLA.
  5. Approve the alpha-site case studies for counsel review → publish.
