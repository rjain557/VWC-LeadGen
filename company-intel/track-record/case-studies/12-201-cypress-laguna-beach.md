# 201 Cypress Dr — Laguna Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **7 units** | **Acquired:** 3/15/2021 | **Sold:** 5/4/2023

## The Opportunity

VWC's third Cypress Drive acquisition in Laguna Beach (after 245 Cypress and 374 Cypress). By March 2021, VWC was the dominant operator on this single street — sourcing, underwriting, and managing the asset alongside two existing portfolio properties within walking distance.

## The Strategy

Acquire, renovate, exit into the strongest Southern California coastal multifamily window in a generation. CapEx of $314K on a $3.15M acquisition (10% of price) — a meaningful interior reposition. Held 26 months from acquisition to sale.
