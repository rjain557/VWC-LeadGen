# 1570 N PCH — Laguna Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **8 units** | **Acquired:** 8/14/2018 | **Sold:** 8/30/2021

## The Opportunity

An eight-unit asset on Pacific Coast Highway, Laguna Beach — VWC's second Laguna acquisition, building on the 245 Cypress entry from earlier in 2018. PCH frontage in Laguna is a permanent location premium that no amount of new construction can replicate.

## The Strategy

Acquire, renovate, hold through the cycle. Like the Olinda Dana Point asset, this property crossed the COVID-19 disruption mid-hold, extending the timeline to 36 months.

## Investor Takeaway

A trophy-location asset that returned principal plus a small gain through a difficult operating window. Light CapEx (only $76K — under 2% of acquisition) reflects an asset that didn't need heavy reposition; the value was in the location and the hold. With 41% leverage, investors weren't exposed to debt service stress when the eviction moratoria hit. Boring, in the way principal protection is supposed to be boring.
