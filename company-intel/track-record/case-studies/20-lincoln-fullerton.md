# 121-131 Lincoln Ave — Fullerton, CA

**Status:** Stabilized | **12 units** | **Acquired:** 8/22/2022

## The Opportunity

A 12-unit, multi-parcel asset in Fullerton — acquired two weeks after the same-city Malden Ave purchase. By August 2022, VWC was running a coordinated Fullerton sub-strategy (Porter 2017 + Malden 2022 + Lincoln 2022) that mirrored the Laguna Beach Cypress Drive cluster from earlier years.

## The Strategy

Acquire the multi-parcel package, renovate across all 12 units, push rents to current market. CapEx of $437K (10.9% of acquisition) covered a moderate-to-heavy reposition.
