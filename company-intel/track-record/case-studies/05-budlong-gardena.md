# 14523 & 14527 Budlong — Gardena, CA

**Status:** Sold | **12 units** | **Acquired:** 7/18/2018 | **Sold:** 12/24/2019

## The Opportunity

A 12-unit, two-parcel acquisition in Gardena — VWC stepping outside its core Orange County footprint into the South Bay. The combined parcels offered scale (two adjacent buildings, single ownership economics) at a sub-institutional price point.

## The Strategy

Renovate both parcels in parallel, push rents across all 12 units, sell as a packaged transaction. CapEx of $310K covered the program. Seventeen-month hold from acquisition to disposition.

## Investor Takeaway

Demonstrates discipline on geography — VWC will leave the home market when the deal economics warrant it, but the unit-level value-add playbook stays the same. Two-parcel deals offer a packaging premium at exit that single-parcel comparable assets don't.
