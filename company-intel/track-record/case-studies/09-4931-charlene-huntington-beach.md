# 4931 Charlene Circle — Huntington Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **6 units** | **Acquired:** 8/28/2019 | **Sold:** 8/3/2022

## The Opportunity

A six-unit property on a quiet Huntington Beach cul-de-sac. The asset itself is unremarkable — what makes this deal worth studying is what came next: VWC acquired the *adjacent identical sister property* (4921 Charlene) 16 months later in December 2020, creating a 12-unit consolidated coastal position with shared driveway and operational economies.

## The Strategy

Renovate, hold through the COVID disruption, exit into the post-pandemic transaction recovery. The 35-month hold reflects the operating environment — but the post-COVID Orange County coastal market rewarded patience.

## Investor Takeaway

A 43% levered return on conservative 39% leverage. Also the entry deal that opened the Charlene Circle micro-position — when its sister property at 4921 came up for sale, VWC was the obvious buyer because they already operated next door. Local density compounds: every acquisition makes the next one easier to underwrite, easier to operate, and easier to source.
