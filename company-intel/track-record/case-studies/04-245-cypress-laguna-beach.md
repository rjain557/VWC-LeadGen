# 245 Cypress Dr — Laguna Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **6 units** | **Acquired:** 6/1/2018 | **Sold:** 7/20/2021

## The Opportunity

VWC's first multifamily acquisition in Laguna Beach — a six-unit asset within walking distance of the downtown art district, beaches, and restaurants. This deal opened a coastal submarket that VWC would return to four more times (374 Cypress, 201 Cypress, 1570 N PCH, and others).

## The Strategy

Extensive interior renovations across 2018-2019 to capture the Laguna Beach lifestyle premium. Held through COVID and the 2020 disruption, then sold to a local investor at $579K/unit — well above the acquisition basis of $475K/unit.

## Investor Takeaway

The deal that established VWC's footprint in Laguna Beach. From the sale press release: *"VisionWise Capital still retains a portfolio of properties in Laguna Beach... within minutes of the former Cypress property."* One acquisition created the local relationships, broker visibility, and operating presence that compounded into a multi-property submarket strategy. Returns are not the only output of a deal — market knowledge is too.
