# 7042 Fenway Dr — Westminster, CA

**Status:** Sold | **10 units** | **Acquired:** 10/2/2018 | **Sold:** 8/5/2021

## The Opportunity

A ten-unit asset in Westminster — sandwiched between Huntington Beach and the Garden Grove rental corridor. Westminster's older multifamily stock, combined with proximity to OC's job centers, makes it consistent value-add territory.

## The Strategy

Standard playbook: renovate exterior + interiors, push rents to market, sell into improved cap-rate environment. CapEx of $224K (7.4% of acquisition) was the operating engine. Held 34 months across COVID — extended timeline, but the disposition still delivered a top-quartile return for the portfolio.
