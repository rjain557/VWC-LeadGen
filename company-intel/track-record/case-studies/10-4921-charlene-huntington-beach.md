# 4921 Charlene Circle — Huntington Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **6 units** | **Acquired:** 12/17/2020 | **Sold:** 12/5/2022

## The Opportunity

The sister building to VWC's 4931 Charlene Circle property — *literally next door, same building footprint, shared driveway*. From the [acquisition press release](../01.21.2021%20VWC%204921%20Charlene%206%20LLC%20Press%20Announcement.pdf): *"The two sister properties share a common driveway as well as the same building footprint and unit configuration."* This is what local market presence buys you.

## The Strategy

VWC walked into this deal with a verbatim playbook: same neighborhood, same product, same renovation scope, same property manager. From acquisition (December 2020) to sale (December 2022) — exactly 24 months. Improvements were completed by June 2021 in time for the summer leasing season. First acquisition for VWC Multifamily Fund IV, third in the Charlene Circle micro-cluster.

## Investor Takeaway

A 51% levered return on a deal where most of the underwriting risk had already been retired by VWC's operating experience next door. The pattern is the asset: when you operate enough Southern California coastal multifamily, sister-property acquisitions become low-risk repeats of work you've already proven. Investors paid for an information advantage, not a market guess.
