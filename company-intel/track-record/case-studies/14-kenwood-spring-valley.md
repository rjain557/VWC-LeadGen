# 9062 Kenwood Dr — Spring Valley, CA

**Status:** Sold | **20 units** | **Acquired:** 12/30/2021

## The Opportunity

VWC's largest single-asset acquisition to that point — a 20-unit property in Spring Valley, an unincorporated San Diego County community east of downtown. Marked the firm's strategic move into San Diego's metro multifamily market alongside the same-day 630 W. Washington Ave acquisition.

## The Strategy

Scale-up of the value-add playbook to a 20-unit asset. CapEx of $297K — a modest reposition relative to deal size, consistent with assets that needed less heavy-lift than the trophy coastal repositions.

## Investor Takeaway

Demonstrates VWC's range — $241K/unit Spring Valley deal economics work just as cleanly as $475K/unit Laguna Beach deal economics, because the underwriting framework is consistent regardless of submarket. Returns are lower than the coastal-OC cluster, but so is the entry basis and the operating risk. Diversification within the multifamily category, not against it.
