# 5050 Hayter Ave — Lakewood, CA

**Status:** Sold | **5 units** | **Acquired:** 6/11/2019 | **Sold:** 5/13/2020

## The Opportunity

A five-unit asset in Lakewood — a Los Angeles suburb VWC entered to demonstrate the breadth of its acquisition reach beyond Orange County. Two- and three-bedroom unit mix, eight garages, on-site laundry. Walk-distance to Lakewood Center Mall.

## The Strategy

The classic 12-month value-add lifecycle. From the [press release](../05.29.2020%20VWC%20Lakewood%205%20Press%20Release%20-%20FINAL.pdf): *"The property was renovated, re-rented and sold with higher market rental rates within 12 months of acquisition, while navigating the challenges imposed by COVID-19."* Sold for $407K/unit — a meaningful step-up from the $336K/unit acquisition basis. Benefitted investors of VWC Orange County Multifamily Fund II.

## Investor Takeaway

The headline return is small, but the story matters: this asset closed in May 2020, weeks into the COVID lockdown, with a 21% project-level return on the unlevered basis (per the original press release). It exemplified Fund II's "life cycle" strategy — buy, renovate, re-rent, sell — executed on schedule even as the rest of the market froze. Discipline beats waiting for the perfect window.
