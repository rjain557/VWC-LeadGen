# 1629 Civic Center Dr — Santa Ana, CA

**Status:** Sold | **14 units** | **Acquired:** 3/18/2022 | **Sold:** 8/8/2022

## The Opportunity

A 14-unit asset in Santa Ana — an OC market with persistent rental demand from the County's largest workforce concentration (Civic Center, hospitals, courts). The acquisition was VWC's first 14-unit deal, scaling unit count without scaling complexity.

## The Strategy

Acquire, stabilize, and *sell quickly* — fewer than 5 months from close to disposition, the shortest hold in the portfolio. Light CapEx ($68K — under 2% of acquisition) signals the asset was largely turnkey at acquisition; the value capture was in the cap rate, not the renovation.

## Investor Takeaway

A 15.65% return earned in five months. Not every VWC deal is a 24-month renovation play — when an opportunistic exit appears mid-cycle, the firm takes it. Speed-to-exit is its own form of risk management: the capital came back to investors and into the next deal before the late-2022 cap-rate widening.
