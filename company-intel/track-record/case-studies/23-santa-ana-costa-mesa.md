# 2312 Santa Ana — Costa Mesa, CA

**Status:** Stabilized | **12 units** | **Acquired:** 8/10/2023

## The Opportunity

A 12-unit asset in Costa Mesa — VWC's first Costa Mesa acquisition, expanding the OC submarket footprint into one of the County's denser infill rental markets. Costa Mesa's mix of South Coast Plaza-area employment, OC Fairgrounds, and proximity to Newport Beach makes it a structural rental demand market.

## The Strategy

The largest single-asset CapEx commitment in the VWC portfolio at acquisition: $890K — 15.6% of the acquisition price. This was a deeper reposition than the typical VWC deal, justified by the Costa Mesa rent ceiling.

## Investor Takeaway

A 19% projected return on a heavier-touch reposition. Costa Mesa is the pattern of VWC scaling up: deeper CapEx, larger equity commitment, conservative leverage (46% LTV), new submarket added to a portfolio that's already operating across Huntington Beach, Laguna Beach, Fullerton, and San Diego. Each new metro extends the franchise.
