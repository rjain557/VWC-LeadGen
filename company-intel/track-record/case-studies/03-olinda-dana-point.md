# 33741 Olinda Dr — Dana Point, CA

**Status:** Sold | **6 units** | **Acquired:** 6/15/2017 | **Sold:** 8/9/2021

## The Opportunity

VWC's entry into Dana Point — a six-unit asset in one of Orange County's premier coastal submarkets. The deal sat alongside VWC's early Huntington Beach work as part of building a coastal-focused portfolio.

## The Strategy

Acquire, renovate, hold through the value-add cycle. The hold period extended longer than typical (50 months) — Olinda was in the portfolio when COVID-19 reshaped operating conditions, and VWC held for stabilization rather than forcing a sale into a disrupted market.

## Investor Takeaway

The most modest realized return in VWC's portfolio — and a useful one to look at directly. Investors got their principal back plus a small gain through a once-in-a-generation operating disruption (COVID, eviction moratoria, California rent control). Conservative 44% leverage meant the deal could absorb a slow exit without forced losses. The story of this asset is principal protection, not yield.
