# 16941 Hoskins Lane — Huntington Beach, CA

**Status:** Sold | **5 units** | **Acquired:** 4/17/2017 | **Sold:** 4/20/2018

## The Opportunity

VWC's first transaction of the modern fund era — a small, sub-institutional five-plex in one of Orange County's most populated and supply-constrained beach cities. At a $2.0M acquisition, the deal sat squarely in the "too small for institutions, too big for most retail" zone where VWC operates by design.

## The Strategy

Acquire below replacement cost, renovate to market standard, and reposition rents in a coastal market where new supply is functionally capped. Total renovation of $171K (8.5% of acquisition) — a light-but-purposeful capital plan. Twelve-month hold from close to sale.

## Investor Takeaway

A textbook one-year value-add lifecycle. Conservative leverage (37% LTV) meant the return was earned through operations and resale execution — not financial engineering. This deal set the template VWC has repeated 22 more times.
