# 374 Cypress Dr — Laguna Beach, CA (Cypress Garden Apartments)

**Status:** Stabilized | **10 units** | **Acquired:** 9/21/2020

## The Opportunity

A 10-unit "trophy" asset in Laguna Beach — originally built in 1948 as the *Cypress Garden Resort Hotel & Apartments*. Located 1,618 feet from Main Beach and 2,268 feet from downtown Laguna's restaurants and art galleries. From the [acquisition press release](../10.22.2020%20VWC%20Cypress%2010%20LLC%20Announcement.pdf): *"a quiet, luxurious hideaway, a stone's throw from the very center of things."*

This was the **first acquisition for VWC Multifamily Fund IV**, which opened to investors September 1, 2020.

## The Strategy

The acquisition itself is a case study in VWC's sourcing discipline. Per the press release: *"VisionWise Capital originally reviewed the property in April 2020 with a strike price that was $1M below the listing price. Patience and perseverance worked in their favor when in July 2020 the Seller became amenable to their offer."*

The broker cited two reasons VWC won the deal: (1) the completeness of the buyer package, and (2) VWC's existing ownership of two other Laguna Beach properties within walking distance. **Submarket density is competitive moat.**

## Investor Takeaway

The highest projected return in the entire VWC portfolio — earned by waiting four months for the right entry price on an asset that never should have been available below the line. The deal also demonstrates the compounding value of submarket density: VWC's existing Laguna position is what closed the deal, not just the offer price.
