# 925 S. Malden Ave — Fullerton, CA

**Status:** Stabilized | **12 units** | **Acquired:** 8/8/2022

## The Opportunity

A 12-unit asset in Fullerton — VWC's return to a market it knew well from the 2017 Porter acquisition five years earlier. Acquired in August 2022 alongside three other deals (Bendicron, Lincoln Ave, and shortly after, Hornblend) in a busy summer of capital deployment.

## The Strategy

Heavier-CapEx reposition than the typical VWC deal — $532K (13.3% of acquisition) signals significant interior and exterior work. The asset is currently stabilized and operating, with VWC holding for cap rate normalization rather than forcing an exit.

## Investor Takeaway

A modest projected return that reflects current market conditions, not the asset itself. The 30%+ rent uplift achieved through reposition (acquisition $/unit to estimated market $/unit) is consistent with the rest of the portfolio — what's compressed is the cap-rate multiplier on exit. With 52% leverage and a stabilized rent roll, VWC has the optionality to hold through the next cap-rate cycle.
