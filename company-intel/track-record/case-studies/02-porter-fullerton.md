# 2630-2636 W Porter — Fullerton, CA

**Status:** Sold | **11 units** | **Acquired:** 10/6/2017 | **Sold:** 11/19/2018

## The Opportunity

An 11-unit infill property in north Orange County. Fullerton's mix of CSU Fullerton enrollment, established commercial corridors, and aging 1960s-era multifamily stock makes it a long-running source of value-add inventory for VWC.

## The Strategy

Standard playbook — acquire, renovate exterior and interiors, push rents to market, sell within 13 months. CapEx of $276K (8.5% of acquisition) covered the unit interiors and curb-appeal improvements needed to support the rent reset.

## Investor Takeaway

Demonstrates VWC's ability to scale the value-add model from 5-unit to 11-unit deals without breaking the timeline. A clean exit, executed in roughly the same one-year window as the smaller Hoskins property — the process is repeatable.
