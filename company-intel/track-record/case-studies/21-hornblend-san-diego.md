# 1215 Hornblend Dr — San Diego, CA

**Status:** Sold | **10 units** | **Acquired:** 10/3/2022

## The Opportunity

A ten-unit asset in San Diego's Pacific Beach submarket — one of the most consistently rent-resilient infill rental markets in Southern California. VWC's third San Diego acquisition (after 9062 Kenwood and 630 W. Washington), continuing the build-out of a Southern California-wide portfolio rather than an OC-only one.

## The Strategy

Renovate, stabilize, exit. CapEx of $231K (6.7% of acquisition) — a lighter CapEx ratio than the heavier 2022 deals, reflecting a property in better entering condition.
